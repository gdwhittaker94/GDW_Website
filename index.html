About Me

Before the year 2023 I had never written a line of code before in my life 😳 From late 2018 up until then I had been working as an English teacher mostly in Burgos, northern Spain - why there is an interesting story!
I've loved Spain ever since I was little and I thoroughly enjoy living here. Therefore, teaching English seemed like the ideal way to both establish myself economically here, as well as do work which I find fulfilling and meaningful.
I was good at teaching! However, after a couple of years of doing the job I realised I was ready to take on something new and move my career in a new direction 💪
That's when, very much by accident, I discovered programming. At first I was unsure about it - "Me, a programmer?" - but the more I learned about it, the more I got into it and wanted to know more. I still remember the thrill I got the first time I wrote "Hello world!" into a basic text editor and seeing it appear as if by magic on an empty browser window - woah! 🤩
I've progressed a lot since then, and yet I know there is still so much more to learn! Learning programming has not been easy at times, however, I'm still here today learning and building because I do genuinely find all this web dev stuff fun, engaging, and enjoyably challenging!
Now, all I want to do is get better and better, and build more and more beautiful and functional websites for people and businesses like you. If you like the sound of that and want to work with me, don't hesitate to get in touch! ⬇️
